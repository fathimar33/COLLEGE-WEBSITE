Procedure for Admission in College

  1. Submit an online application through the college's website or in person at the admissions office.
  2. Pay the non-refundable application fee.
  3. Submit official transcripts from all previous schools attended.
  4. Provide test scores for any required entrance exams, such as the SAT or ACT.
  5. Submit a personal statement or essay, if required by the college.
  6. Submit letters of recommendation, if required by the college.
  7. Participate in an interview or attend an information session, if required by the college.
  8. Wait for the college's admissions decision, which will be sent by mail or email.

Note: This is a general guideline and the specific admission procedure may vary based on the college. Be sure to check the college's website or contact the admissions office for more information.
